Itemized expense

Click a person's cell to toggle them in or out of an item — the cost splits evenly, with cents balanced to the row total. Use the checkbox on the right to include everyone, or uncheck it to clear a row (then click just the person who paid). In the PWD / Senior row, type the discountable amount (the VAT-exclusive base the restaurant discounted — the receipt's DISCOUNTABLE line) and click the PWD person; the VAT-exempt + discount deduction goes entirely to them, and the service charge (Tip) is computed on the VAT-exclusive net. Saved locally in your browser.
